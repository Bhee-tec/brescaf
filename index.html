At the Breast Feed the Child and Peace Advocate Foundation, we believe in nurturing the next generation from the very start. Our mission is to promote the well-being of children through the natural and essential act of breast feeding the child, while also advocating for a world built on peace, compassion, and unity.
Our mission is to: Educate mothers and families on the benefits of breast feeding the child, providing resources that help in overcoming challenges. Advocate for the normalization of breast feeding in public spaces and workplaces, ensuring that all mothers can breast feed their children comfortably. Promote peace and conflict resolution in families, encouraging a culture of kindness, respect, and empathy from an early age.

What We Do?

THE AIMS AND OBJECTIVES OF THE ASSOCIATION

To provide sustainable strategy for poverty allevation

Brescaf is committed to sustainable poverty alleviation through education, entrepreneurship, and community empowerment. We provide skills training, microfinance support, and access to essential resources, fostering self-reliance and long-term economic stability. Through innovation and collaboration, we create lasting solutions that uplift individuals and communities, breaking the cycle of poverty

To create psychiatric care and education

Brescaf is dedicated to providing accessible psychiatric care and mental health education. We offer counseling, awareness programs, and community support to promote emotional well-being. Through advocacy, research, and training, we empower individuals and families with the knowledge and resources needed to overcome mental health challenges and lead fulfilling lives.

To advocate for peace

Brescaf is committed to advocating for peace through dialogue, education, and community engagement. We promote conflict resolution, social harmony, and mutual understanding by fostering peaceful coexistence. Through advocacy, workshops, and outreach programs, we empower individuals and communities to embrace nonviolence, unity, and sustainable solutions for a more peaceful world

Orphan widows and widower empowerment

Brescaf is dedicated to empowering orphans, widows, and widowers through education, skills training, and economic support. We provide mentorship, financial aid, and livelihood programs to foster self-sufficiency and emotional well-being. Our mission is to uplift vulnerable individuals, giving them hope, dignity, and the tools to build a better future.

Youth and woman empowerment

Brescaf is committed to youth and women empowerment through education, skills training, and entrepreneurship. We provide mentorship, financial support, and leadership programs to foster self-reliance and economic independence. By equipping individuals with the right tools, we create opportunities for growth, innovation, and a brighter future for communities.

Mental Health Support for Orphans and Vulnerable Children in Nigeria

The USAID-funded ACHIEVE project has been instrumental in building mental health and psychosocial support capacity for orphans and vulnerable children in Nigeria. This initiative underscores the critical need for continued support in mental health services for these groups.

Global Call to Protect Women's Rights in Conflict Zones

An open letter to the United Nations urges decisive action to safeguard women's rights amid global conflicts and crises. This highlights the ongoing challenges women face in conflict areas and the necessity for advocacy and intervention.

Economic Empowerment Programs Benefiting Nigerian Youth and Women

The Managing Conflict in Nigeria (MCN) program has positively impacted communities by providing youth and women with skills training in various trades, promoting economic independence and reducing conflict.

Advancing Women's Roles in Peacebuilding Efforts

The United Nations Development Programme (UNDP) collaborates with women leaders and peacebuilders to equip them with tools to address trauma and contribute to peacebuilding, emphasizing the vital role women play in fostering peace.
